Use Cases

Use case 1:

Hovering: Drones can hover! Isn't that neat? Quadcopters can typically spin their propellers at a somewhat even pace and use a gyro to adjust for unbalances, allowing them to hover.

Use case 2:

Move: Quadcopter drones can typically fly in different directions based on user input, usually bieng faster or slower and if they are more sophsticated they can keep stable for reasonable distances.

Special use case:

Gacha machine: A special use case for the drones is a gacha machine/claw machine/UFO Catcher. The idea is that you can have a bunch of items in an area and use the drone to try to pick them up into a box, and if you put it in the box you "win" the item, just like a claw machine in an amusement center. You could have a claw attatchment that closes and use the drone as a mechanism to go higher or lower.
